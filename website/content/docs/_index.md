Welcome to the documentation for **Guido's Helping Hand** -- an automated
pipeline for processing photographed pages of historical music books into
clean, searchable PDFs.

Whether you are a conservationist digitizing a cathedral archive, a scholar
preparing a critical edition, or a musician seeking a readable copy of a
Gregorian chant manuscript, these guides will help you get the most out of
the tool.
